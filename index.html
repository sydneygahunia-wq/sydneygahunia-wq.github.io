PASSENGER PROFILE — WHO I AM

TRAVELLER. BUILDER. PROBLEM-SOLVER.

I'm Sydney — a Systems Design Engineering student at the University of Waterloo who likes building things that make everyday systems easier to use. Most recently, that meant designing AI chatbot and voice-assistant prototypes at Humber River Health to help hospital staff get technical support faster and make it easier for callers to reach the right department.

I'm especially interested in human-centred AI and healthcare technology. That interest also led me to build an app inspired by my own experience with supraventricular tachycardia (SVT), designed to help patients track and better understand their heart episodes.

Off the clock, I'm usually travelling somewhere new, leading STEM workshops, or showing prospective students around Waterloo Engineering as a faculty ambassador.
